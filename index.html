Disclaimer Section

Grade Meaning

Grades are a quick summary of a card's power level.
The grades are as follows:

A:

Bomb. Always play and should strongly pull you in a color.

B:

Really Good. Not exactly a bomb but good enough to pull you in a color.

C:

Playable. These cards are not bad but not great. Once you have a color, look to these to fill out the rest of your deck.

D:

Filler. Your deck needs to be at least 40 cards and these cards help you get there.

F:

Trash. Don't Play.

S/B:

Sideboard. Pretty self-explanatory, cards that are good in the sideboard.

Build:

These cards are linear and can only be graded in decks that are built around this card. Power level drops significantly in decks not built around these cards.
